# Ascent Modal Enhancements

## 1. "Tomorrow is Summit Day" Warning — ALL Tiers
- Currently: only shows on Avalanche Warning tier
- **Change**: Show on ANY pacing tier on the day before Summit Day
- Summit Bound, Off the Trail, Lost in the Woods, Rockslide, Avalanche — ALL get the "⚠️ Tomorrow is Summit Day" banner
- This is a heads-up, not exclusively a bad-pacing warning

## 2. "Today is Summit Day" Warning — ALL Tiers (NEW)
- **New banner**: "🏔️ Today is Summit Day!"
- Shows on ANY pacing tier when today = Summit Day
- Summit Bound, Off the Trail, Rockslide, Avalanche — all get this
- If they don't finish today → Anchor Failure triggers the next day
- The modal they get ON Summit Day is predictive of what comes next

## 3. Approach Completion Indicator on ALL Ascent Modals
- **Two states:**

### A) Approach COMPLETE (at any time — Day 5, 6, 7, 15, 18, whenever):
- Green pill/indicator showing something like "✅ Approach Complete" or "✅ Approached the Mountain"
- Simple visual confirmation they finished Week 1

### B) Approach NOT COMPLETE:
- **Rule: Cannot achieve Summit Reached if Approach + Ascent aren't both done**
- Incomplete Approach modules get added to the "Catch Up" list
- Section becomes "Modules + Clips to Catch Up On"
- Approach items marked with 🚡 emoji to distinguish them from Ascent clips
- Example:
  - 🚡 Challenger sign-off
  - 🚡 Wheel & Deal
  - 🎬 Week 3 Day 12: Customer Stories
  - 🎬 Week 3 Day 13: Contract Lifecycle
